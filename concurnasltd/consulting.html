Consulting

We provide access to consultants who are experienced hands on lead engineers, project managers and system architects who are practiced in modern software development methodologies including Agile, DevOps, Kanban, Scrum, Six Sigma etc.

We set the bar very high for employees of Concurnas. All of our consultants are expected to be hands on contributors to the Concurnas programming language. As such, post orientation they are generally able to hit the ground running and start contributing to your organization immediately. You are welcome to review our careers page for more details of our high standards.

We will work with you throughout all stages of our interaction (pre, during and post contract) to ensure there is a good fit between your organization and the consultant(s) we provide

Q. What languages/technologies do you cover?

All of our consultant engineers are polyglot programmers and contributers to The Concurnas Programming language. We're able to consult principally for Concurnas but also in any other language such as Java, C/C++, Python, Javascript, Kotlin, Scala, Matlab, PHP, SQL, Julia, KDB/Q, Erlang, Haskel, SWIFT, Go etc, you name it or even you're own DSL!

Although our consultants are experts in Concurnas, we will always strive to recommend the best and most appropriate technology for your needs.

Q. What can you help my organization with?

We can help you with the following including (but not limited to):

  • Software design and development

  • Technology leadership: project management, lead developers

  • Integrations with third party software

  • Design and code review

  • Performance engineering, benchmarking, scalability

  • Test engineering

Q. What industries/areas of technology do you cover?

We are able to offer services to all industries but we can offer specialized expertise in the following areas:

  • Concurrent and distributed systems

    Having built an open source programming language designed for building scalable, high performance concurrent, distributed and parallel systems from scratch our consultants are uniquely positioned to assist you in building these sorts of complex system

  • Financial Technology

    High frequency trading systems, derivative trading systems, algorithmic/systematic trading systems, low latency programming, GPU programming

  • Big data

    Analytics, processing and management

  • Domain Specific Languages

    We can help you build your own domain specific (or even general purpose!) programming languages, either using Concurnas or other platforms.

Q. Can your consultants work remotely or on location?

Most of our consultants can either work remotely or on location (currently London, UK only).

Q. What length of contract do you support?

The minimum contract duration for which we are able to offer consultants is 3 months.

Q. What is the employment status of your consultants?

All of our consultants are employees of Concurnas Ltd. We will work with you to ensure that our contracts are outside of UK IR35 legislation where relevant.

Q. What are your fees?

Fees are agreed on a per client, per contract basis. We aim to offer a premium service to industry leading clients and our fees are reflective of this. Contact us to open up a discussion today!
